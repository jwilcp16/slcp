The team which enjoys beautiful Colorado Springs with NO MONEY

Who We Are

We are the Colorado Springs. Mt team is made up of Chris Phelps and Nick Angel (Linux), Alex Truxess McGaffic and Gus Urioste (Windows), and Kian Attari and Eoin Mason (Cisco). Like #wearelegion, our team is also largely made up of Cyber Patrol veterans. It's our mission, and we're not quite succeeding, to beat our rival team #wearelegion. We love showing up for every meeting and competition and learning about what it takes to run a secure network.

What CyberPatriot Means To Us

Cyber Patriot means a lot of different things to our team. For me (Gus), it gave me a chance to step up and become a leader. For all of us, the competition is a chance to learn new skills and interact with new people. It means getting a chance to advance in the world doing things we like doing and finding out more about what is interesting to us. Cyber Patriot has been a great opportunity and we are thankful for all the doors this competition has opened for us.
