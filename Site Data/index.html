The South Lakes Cyber Patrol Club

Training our future to be prepared for an increasingly connected world.

In the modern, connected world we live in today, it is critical that our young people are sent out prepared for the threats associated with this new lifestyle, be it basic online safety techniques, like using strong passwords, or something more intricate, like securing a vulnerable workstation.

Regardless of what the task may be, it is the goal of the SLCP to ensure that all members and participants can handle it. Using the training scenarios in the CyberPatriot competition, we can track member knowledge and progress in a way that is both engaging and fun.

Learning and Working Together

Engaging and working with each other.

Despite being in competition, teams are never hesitant to help one another. Through this lack of animosity, and constant cooperation, learning experiences are more wide-reaching and impactful.

Collaborating with Professionals

Not only working with each other, we partner with companies and professionals.

By partnering with those already in the industries and environments, our members are given a sense of what the field of Cybersecurity is like, and learn how to become an effective resource for governments, companies, and others.
