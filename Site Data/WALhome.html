We Are Legion

The members, music choice, and more of the Legion

The Legacy Team

We Are Legion is the team comprising of most of last year's veterans. With an excellent balance of team skills, WAL can quickly amd efficiently work on Windows, Linux, Cisco, and other challenges that present themselves. However, the Legion is not a purely competitive group. They often help the other teams, as much of the other teams are new to the CyberPatriot program. Simultaniously training, teaching, and leading, We Are Legion is truly the jack-of-all-trades in the South Lakes Cyber Patrol.

The members of We Are Legion all are competent in all areas, but also have specialties. The members are Jonas, Jason, Joseph, Zach, Ahmed, and Kenny. Joseph and Jonas are the team's specialists for Linux and Cisco challenges, both training in each category. Jason, Ahmed, and Kenny have a primary focus of Windows-based images and challenges. Zach and Jason also are in charge of images for Windows Server.

Member Bios

Though all have a common interest in cybersecurity, the Legion has a very diverse range of interests. Jason, Ahmed, Kenny, and Joseph are all avid programmers. Zach and Jonas enjoy urban exploration. The official song of We Are Legion is the only reasonable thing that could be played when facing them; Kenny Loggins' Danger Zone
